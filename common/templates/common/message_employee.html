3. Proceed to fill out the forms using your personal information.

You must confirm at least the following information in order validate the questionnaire: {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% trans 'Last Name' %}

  • {% trans 'First Name' %}

  • Email

  • {% trans 'Date of Birth' %}

{% blocktrans %} The field « Foreigner ID » corresponds to the 10 digit number written on the middle right side of your residence permit or on the OFII validation documents. If you do not hold a French residence permit yet, please ignore this field.
